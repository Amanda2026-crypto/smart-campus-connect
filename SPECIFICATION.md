# 📋 System Specification Document

## Smart Campus Connect - Student Lifecycle Management System

---

## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Project Title
**Smart Campus Connect (SCC)**

### 1.2 Domain
**Education Technology (EdTech) / Campus Management**

Smart Campus Connect operates in the Education Technology domain, focusing on enhancing student experience through digital transformation of campus life.

### 1.3 Problem Statement

Today's university students face a fragmented digital experience:
- Students juggle 5-7 different apps for courses, events, meals, etc.
- Important information gets lost across multiple channels
- Students miss events because discovery is poor
- Finding study spaces requires walking around campus
- Lost items have no centralized reporting system

**Solution:** Smart Campus Connect creates one unified platform for all student needs.

### 1.4 Individual Scope & Feasibility

This project is achievable in one semester because:
- Modular design allows building one feature at a time
- You understand student needs firsthand
- Many modules follow similar patterns
- Can use existing APIs for maps, payments, etc.
